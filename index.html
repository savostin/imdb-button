Rating Button

The missed old good IMDb rating button.
Now in modern style.

Features:

  • Vector (SVG). Scale to any size.
  • Shows current rating and number of votes.
  • Linked to the film page on the IMDb site (no referer, no opener!)
  • Caches info for 12 hours to prevent overloading.
  • More to come...

Examples:

  • The Shawshank Redemption
  • Top Gun: Maverick
  • Daniel the Wizard
  • Jaws: The Revenge
  • F9: The Fast Saga

How to use:

  1. Put this code on your page:
    <a href="https://imdb.com/title/[IMDb ID]" referrerpolicy="no-referrer" target="_blank"><img src="https://rating.rumdb.com/[IMDb ID].svg" /></a>
    where [IMDb ID] is the ID, like "tt12345678".
  2. Enjoy.
